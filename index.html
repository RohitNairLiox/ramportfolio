WHO IS HE?


Ram Subramanian is multi-talented personality who uses communication to constantly redefine our society. He has built brands, created new ideas, broken old rules, initiated movements and supported social causes. Some of his work has become case studies and some have become the new normal in advertising and communication, around the world. Ram heads Handloom Picture Company, a production house that owns “Voice Of Ram”, a powerful online platform, with a huge following.


Go to: Handloom Picture Company / Voice Of Ram




WHAT HAS HE DONE?


Ram Subramanian has produced hundreds of viral messages and campaigns in the past few years. His repertoire, as a filmmaker, includes artistic TV commercials, powerful short-films and emotional social-change videos.
As a peace activist, he has ignited many iconic mass movements like the DearNeighbour Movement, #ProfileForPeace and created personalities like ‘Gurmehar Kaur – A martyr’s daughter’ that made headlines all around the world. As a political/communication strategist, Ram played an instrumental role in building Arvind Kejriwal’s AAP and helping the political outfit create history by winning an unprecedented 67 out of 70 seats in 2014 Delhi elections. Some of his work for AAP is their communication template even today.
