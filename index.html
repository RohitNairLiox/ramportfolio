  • Founded Voice of Ram

    2016 - present

    ♦ Started #ProfileForPeace campaign to promote good ties between India and Pakistan at an individual level. This campaign became a viral hit in both the nations and motivated citizens of both the nations to start a dialogue with each other. This campaign was joined by various celebrities from both the nations. To name some:Vishal Dadlani from India, Rehan Allahwalla from Pakistan etc

    ♦ Started #ProfileForPeace - T20 World Cup Campaign. Under this people from both the nations came forward to support each other's Cricket team

    ♦ Directed #ProfileForPeace Video (Gurmehar Kaur)

    ♦ Directed #IAmTeamIndia video. It featured Saina Nehwal and carried a message for India before Rio Olympics

    ♦ Peace Up video featured Ram Subramanian, was directed by himself. The video carried a message of peace for the people of India & Pakistan

    ♦ #KillTerroristsNotTalks campaign was started by Ram Subramanian to appeal to the government of both the nation to take a diplomatic path to curb terrorism instead of breaking ties with each other. This campaign became a hot topic in both the nations as this time too, many renowned people like Mahesh Bhatt joined in

    ♦ Ram Subramanian started 'The Green Project' to bring a small change in the teaching system of India

    2017


    ♦ During BMC elections of 2017, Ram Subramanian came forward to educate people about the cutting of Aarey Forests in and around Mumbai and to ask them to vote responsibly. The campaign was known as #VoteToSaveMumbai #VoteToSaveAarey

    ♦ #StudentsAgainstABVP, the campaign which started after DU row in 2017 was initialy guided by Ram Subramanian. The campaign was supported students from various universities of India and led to a mass protest in Delhi

    ♦ #LetsUncolour campaign was started to educate people about and to raise voice against commercially induced racism in Indian and other South Asian societies. The campaign caught the eye of media and was supported by various renowned people. This campaign is still in process

    ♦ The Red Project initiative was started with the motive to connect blood donors in India and Pakistan based upon their city and blood group
